Why Partner with Us

Consistency You Can Trust

Pre-production samples match bulk exactly &mdash; this is our non-negotiable. We enforce a rigorous 3-stage QC protocol to eliminate sizing deviation, color drift, and fabric shrinkage issues that plague cross-border sourcing. Every production batch is measured against the approved sealed sample, so what you approve is what you receive. No more complaints about patterns going off-spec or garments shrinking after wash.

Speed and Scale, Balanced

Quality, speed, and cost are often considered an impossible triangle. With 15,000 sqm of production floorspace, 500+ skilled workers, and dual-hub JIT operations, we find the balance point that fits your specific program &mdash; whether you need agile small-batch testing or massive bulk delivery. Our scale keeps unit costs competitive; our specialization keeps quality uncompromised. We design production plans around your actual needs, not a one-size-fits-all formula.

Full Journey, One Partner

From product development, fabric sourcing, and first-fit sampling to bulk production, finishing, and QC dispatch &mdash; we cover the entire journey under one roof. No handoffs between separate vendors, no accountability gaps. We design production plans around your specific requirements, supported by OEKO-TEX certified fabric channels and open-door 3rd-party QC access including SGS and Intertek.

20+ Years of Manufacturing Evolution

2005 — 2010

From a Single Sample Room and Boutique

The group began with one specialized garment sample room and a single retail storefront, building hands-on expertise in pattern making, fabric selection, and construction quality from the ground up.

2011 — 2015

OEM Division Formed &mdash; RMB 100M Annual Output

Established the Dongguan OEM Manufacturing Division, scaling from sample-room precision to industrial production lines. By 2015, the division reached a landmark annual output value of 100 million RMB, driven by exacting international QC standards and repeat orders from global buyers.

2019

ODM Design and E-Commerce Division Launched

Built an in-house design-driven ODM R&D team to serve high-growth cross-border e-commerce platforms. Launched proprietary seasonal collections, shifting from pure contract manufacturing to full-spectrum design-to-delivery partnerships for online brands, wholesalers, and trading partners.

2020

Guangxi Volume-Production Base and Smart Apparel Incubation

Opened the Guangxi Mass-Production Division, purpose-built for high-volume knit and woven apparel programs. In parallel, incubated a Smart Apparel and Wearables Division, launching the proprietary Warma series of heated garments and intelligent wearable devices.

2023

Fast-Turn Division Scaled to 300K Units/Month

Expanded the Dongguan agile division into a digitally-managed fast-turn powerhouse. A single workshop now consistently delivers 300,000 units per month, supporting rapid reorders and trend-chasing replenishment for global supply chains.

2026

In-House Digital Printing and Laser Finishing Division

Integrated a wholly-owned digital textile printing and laser finishing division, operating industrial-grade sublimation transfer, all-over full-width printing, and precision laser burnout. Capable of processing knit, woven, mesh, plush, and blended fabrics with a monthly output of 800,000 meters.

Dual-Hub Manufacturing Strategy

Dongguan HQ

The Agile Center

Capable of both knit and woven production, the Dongguan hub is optimized for small-to-mid batch agility, covering fast prototyping, rapid replenishment, and complex ODM development. Operates manual precision lines and fully-automated production cells under a JIT management model, serving cross-border e-commerce, fast-fashion brands, wholesalers, and trading partners.

Guangxi Branch

Volume Production Base

Also handling both knit and woven construction, the Guangxi hub is engineered for high-volume, massive-scale stability. Combining automated mass-production lines with dedicated manual finishing stations, it delivers global cost-efficiency and reliable lead-times for wholesalers, trading companies, and slow-fashion brands seeking long-running program consistency.

In-House Technical Capabilities

6 Specialized Sewing Workshops

Six dedicated production workshops, each focused on a specific garment category, enabling specialized craftsmanship and streamlined management per product type. Spanning knit and woven construction across the full range: dresses, shirts, blouses, T-shirts, trousers, and multi-layered skirts. Capable of handling mesh, lace, and blended fabrics with dedicated washing, rivet-fastening, and bartack reinforcement stations on each line.

In-House Digital Print and Laser Finishing Center

Wholly-owned digital sublimation transfer, all-over full-width printing, and precision laser burnout &mdash; 800,000 meters monthly capacity. Directly processes knit, woven, mesh, and plush fabrics for high-definition, color-accurate results with no outsourcing delays.

Specialized Value-Add Process Hub

In-house specialist stations for precision mechanical smocking, multi-head computerized embroidery, automated high-fix rhinestone application, industrial laser burnout, garment washing, and rivet-fastening. Smart-heated element embedding capabilities also serve functional wearables programs.
